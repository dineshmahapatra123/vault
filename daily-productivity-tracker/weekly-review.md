# Weekly Review

**Cadence:** Every Friday at 15:00
**Attendees:** Self, asynchronous update to [[manager]]

The weekly review is the immune system of the [[daily-productivity-tracker]]. It cleans out the inboxes, reviews the week's output, and sets the stage for Monday.

## 1. Clear 
- [ ] Process physical inbox / notebook
- [ ] Achieve Inbox Zero in email
- [ ] Close all open browser tabs
- [ ] Review the [[habits-tracker]]

## 2. Get Current
- [ ] Review the past week's [[daily-log]] entries: Did we actually do deep work?
- [ ] Review upcoming calendar for next week with [[assistant]]
- [ ] Move any lingering tasks to next week or delete them

## 3. Get Creative
- [ ] What is one bold idea to pitch to [[manager]] next week?
- [ ] How can we improve the [[time-blocking-strategy]]?
