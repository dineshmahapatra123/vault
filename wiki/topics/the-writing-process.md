# The Writing Process

## What's the Story
The diverse and often idiosyncratic methods writers use to trick themselves into confidence and finish a project. While "craft" is often discussed in abstract or academic terms, practiced writers often describe it as a "private language" or a set of "fraudulent" tactics used to manage the psychological difficulty of creation.

## Key Arguments
- **Macro vs. Micro**: The choice between pre-planning structural security and floor-by-floor construction.
- **The First Draft Hurdle**: The existential drama of finding a "tonal frequency" in the beginning of a work.
- **Magical Thinking**: The middle-of-the-project state where the world feeds into the narrative.
- **Distance as Editor**: The necessity of becoming a "smart stranger" to one's own work through time and separation.
