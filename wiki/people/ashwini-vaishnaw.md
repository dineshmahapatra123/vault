# Ashwini Vaishnaw

## Role/Background
Union Minister of India holding three portfolios: Railways; Information & Broadcasting; and Electronics & Information Technology. A technocrat-turned-politician, he has been the face of India's infrastructure modernisation drive under the current government.

## Known For
Overseeing the largest peacetime transformation of Indian Railways — track renewal, mechanised maintenance, the Vande Bharat Express rollout, and a dramatic reduction in consequential accidents. Also associated with pushing India's semiconductor and electronics manufacturing ambitions.
