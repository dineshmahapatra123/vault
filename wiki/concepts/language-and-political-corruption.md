# Language and Political Corruption

**Definition:** The thesis — most fully developed by George Orwell in "Politics and the English Language" (1946) — that vague, abstract, and euphemistic language is not merely bad style but a *political instrument*. When language becomes imprecise, it becomes easier to defend the indefensible, conceal atrocities, and prevent clear thought. The decay of language and the decay of politics are mutually reinforcing.

**Why It Matters:** This is one of the most practically important ideas in political writing. It explains why bureaucratic language, political speeches, and institutional communication tend toward fog — not by accident, but by function. Fog *serves* power. And conversely: clarity is a radical act.

**The Core Mechanism:**
The causal chain runs both ways:
1. Foolish or corrupt *thought* → vague *language* (to disguise the thought)
2. Vague *language* → easier to have foolish or corrupt *thought* (the language does the thinking)

Orwell: *"A man may take to drink because he feels himself to be a failure, and then fail all the more completely because he drinks."* Language corruption works the same way — it reinforces its own cause.

**The Four Vices of Bad Political Writing (Orwell's taxonomy):**
| Vice | What It Is | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Dying Metaphors | Clichés used without thinking | "toe the line," "hotbed," "swan song" |
| Verbal False Limbs | Phrases that eliminate simple verbs | "render inoperative" instead of "break" |
| Pretentious Diction | Latin/Greek words to create false profundity | "utilize" instead of "use" |
| Meaningless Words | Political words with no fixed meaning | "democracy," "freedom," "fascist" |

**The Political Consequence:**
In Orwell's sharpest passage: *"Political language is designed to make lies sound truthful and murder respectable, and to give an appearance of solidity to pure wind."*

Modern examples:
- "Enhanced interrogation" = torture
- "Collateral damage" = civilian deaths
- "Restructuring" = mass layoffs
- "Transfer of population" = ethnic cleansing
